About Us

We are specialist tour planners for tourists in Rwanda. Our team has decades of combined travel experience in the area and we have personally visited and approved all of the destinations we recommend. Tour Rwanda was established by Rwandese who previously worked as a tour guide in Uganda and Rwanda . The main gateway to the home of the last remaining mountain Gorillas. At Tour Rwanda we pride ourselves on crafting itineraries that are tailored specifically for each client’s requirements and budgets. Our creative ideas, high level of personal service and attention to detail has earned us the loyalty of a number of blue chip companies who have used our services for incentive trips and conferences throughout Rwanda.
