    About the Hotel

    Golden Tulip La Palisse Kigali Hotel is a luxury hotel situated in the Nyamata area in Rwanda. Along with an ultra modern meeting/conference facility, this hotel features a business centre, a tastefully designed restaurant, fully furnished rooms and an onsite bar. Free WiFi is available within the premises.

    The elegantly furnished rooms are designed with sitting areas fitted with plush sofa and en-suite bathrooms with cubicle showers, heaters and hairdryers. Each room features an ornate lampshade, an air conditioner, artistic wall arts, a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV with satellite reception, a work desk and bed-side table.

    The onsite restaurant which is fitted with four-sitter tables serves a wide range of local and continental meals, while guests order alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks at the bar. Outdoor dining is available and breakfast is complimentary.

    The hotel features a business centre and a conference facility which is ideal for workshops, lectures and seminars. Laundry, airport pickup, car hire, babysitting and concierge service are provided upon request. Onsite parking is available and the premises are guarded by trained security personnel.

    Golden Tulip La Palisse Kigali Hotel is 20 kilometers from the city centre of Kigali and the main business area, international organizations and embassies, while the airport is 25 km from the hotel on the outskirts of the city.
