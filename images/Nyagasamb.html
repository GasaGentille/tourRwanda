Description

Nyagasambu Realm Beach Hotel has a garden, bar, a shared lounge and water sports facilities in Janjagiro. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and a shared kitchen, along with free WiFi. Extra facilities include room service, meeting rooms, a tour desk and ironing service. The rooms in the hotel are fitted with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchen, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a bath and a hot tub. The units will provide guests with a desk and a kettle. Guests at Nyagasambu Realm Beach Hotel can enjoy a continental breakfast. The accommodation offers a children's playground. Bike hire and car hire are available at Nyagasambu Realm Beach Hotel and the area is popular for diving and fishing. Number of rooms : 3
